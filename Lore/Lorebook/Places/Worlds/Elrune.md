[ENTRY: Elrune]

#### [World]
**Name:** Elrune  
**Summary:** Primary world shaping realms, trade, and conflict within a connected, storied setting.
**Full Description:** Elrune anchors the known lands with defined realms like Greenwood and the Rootlands. Travel and trade depend on river corridors, mountain passes, canopy crownways, and subterranean roads. Blight outbreaks alter passages, shaping practical routes. Governance, culture, and magic vary per realm; caravans and couriers link disparate regions.
**local inhabitants:** None  
**Tone Keywords:** vast, connected, mapped, storied  
**Locations of Note:** Greenwood, The Rootlands, The Southlands  

**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Plot a route that safely navigates between realms during Blight surges.
- Investigate raiding incidents disrupting travel and trade.
