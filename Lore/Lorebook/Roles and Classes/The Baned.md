### The Baned

[ENTRY: The Baned]

#### [Class]
**Name:** The Baned  
**Summary:** Permanently altered by advanced Blight, the Baned sense infection, resist stronger strains, and often serve as Wardens despite social stigma.  
**Full Description:** The Baned are individuals whose Blight infection advanced beyond timely treatment, leaving permanent physiological and psychological alteration. While specialized potions and talismans can halt further progression and suppress transmissibility, the underlying corruption remains. Baned gain an uncanny ability to sense other infected beings and exhibit resistance to stronger strains circulating in the Forsaken Lands. Ostracized by most communities, many channel their hardened resilience into service as Wardens, leveraging their senses in the fight against the spreading corruption.  
**Tone Keywords:** scarred, vigilant, resilient, stigmatized  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Track an outbreak using Baned senses while avoiding panic in a settlement.  
- Retrieve reagents for stabilizing talismans before symptoms surge.  
- Serve as liaison between Wardens and a fearful village after exposure.
