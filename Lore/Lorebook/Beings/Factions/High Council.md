### High Council
Formed to govern Verdant Hall and its surrounding villages, the Council's authority relied on the Varden Rangers' reputation and reach. By the early 500s:AF corruption and distance from settlements eroded support, giving Malakai Ashwood room to build a loyal bloc within the Rangers. In 639:AF his faction overthrew the Council and executed most members, accelerating Greenwood's fracture in the decades before the Shattering.
