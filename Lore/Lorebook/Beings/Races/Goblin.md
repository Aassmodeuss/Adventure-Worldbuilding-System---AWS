#### [Race]
**Name:** Goblin
**Aliases:** None known
**Summary:** Goblins are a small, thin race known for cruelty and cunning, most often found in caves or deep underground.
**Full Description:** Goblins are a diminutive and slender species that typically inhabit caves or subterranean environments. Their behavior is marked by cruelty and cunning. Goblins often take pleasure in causing pain and suffering to others. They are recognized as the natural enemies of Dwarves in all regions. Goblin societies focus on survival and the exploitation of weaker creatures. Peaceful interactions with other races are uncommon for goblins. Their underground dwellings are difficult to find and hazardous to approach. Goblins usually live 60 to 80 years, reaching adulthood at 18 and elder status at 65.
**Tone Keywords:** objective, concise, factual
**Typical Lifespan:** 60-80 years; adulthood at 18; elder at 60
**Stature and Build:** Average height, medium build
**Primary Regions:** Central Plains, River Valleys
**Aligned Factions:** Council of Elders
**Notable Characters:** Elder Marik, Scout Lira
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**
- Seasonal migration disrupts trade routes
- Council disputes over river access escalate
- Elder succession triggers factional tension
