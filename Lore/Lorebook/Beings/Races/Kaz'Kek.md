**Name:** Kaz'Kek

**Summary:** The Kaz'Kek are small, color-changing lizardfolk who inhabit the high forests near the eastern Stormpeak foothills. They are known for their caution and arboreal lifestyle.
**Full Description:** Kaz'Kek are a diminutive lizardfolk race residing in the high forest canopies along the eastern Stormpeak foothills. Their tribal dwellings are constructed above ground to avoid threats. Each individual stands about waist-high to an average human and possesses a long prehensile tail. Kaz'Kek can alter their skin color to communicate mood or blend with their environment. This ability aids both social interaction and camouflage. Persistent dangers from raiders, slavers, and predators have fostered a culture of wariness and distrust toward outsiders. Kaz'Kek social groups are tightly knit and rarely welcome strangers.
