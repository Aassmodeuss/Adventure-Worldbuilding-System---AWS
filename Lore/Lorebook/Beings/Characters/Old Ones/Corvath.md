#### [Character]
**Name:** Corvath the Raven King  
**Aliases:** The Harbinger of Whispers, Keeper of Echoes  
**Summary:**  Corvath embodies wit, memory, and the dark humour of truth. He is the Old One who carries messages between spirits and mortals.  
**Full Description:**  An ancient raven towering nearly five feet tall, Corvath's iridescent feathers shimmer violet beneath moonlight. Companion to Fenril the Great Wolf, he is often the first sign of the Old Ones' attention in mortal affairs. Playful yet piercing, his laughter precedes omens, and his eyes reflect knowledge too heavy for most to bear. Priests and shamans invoke his name for guidance when truth must be told without mercy.  
**Tone Keywords:** wit, foresight, memory, mischief, revelation  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Appears as an omen or guide during spirit-realm transitions or key revelations.  
- Teaches rituals allowing communication with the spirits of the Old Ones.  
- His feathers may serve as rare reagents for truth-binding charms or divination rites.
