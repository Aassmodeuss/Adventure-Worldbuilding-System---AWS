#### [Character]
**Name:** Helja  
**Aliases:** Canopy Healer, Witch of Westwood  
**Summary:**  A fey shaman in the Westwood canopy, Helja treats the Baned and trades remedies for service. Her aid is fair, her prices exact, and lies do not pass her door.  
**Full Description:**  Helja lives in a treetop house ringed with rare plants and bone talismans. Tall and sure-footed, she bears emerald skin, black eyes, and silver white hair braided with herbs and charms. She cultivates fungi, venoms, and warding vine for poultices and talismans. She treats Baned with early signs of blight infection. Clients who try to cheat her face unique and creative punishments that leave lasting lessons.  
**Tone Keywords:** restorative, shrewd, feycraft, boundary, consequence  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Secure a rare reagent from Shadowlands margins, earn a Baned suppressant or ward talisman.  
- Submit to a truth test to buy a cure, fail and accept a binding favor as restitution.  
- Escort Baned refugees to her canopy rooms, defend the line from leechvines and syndicate tolls.
