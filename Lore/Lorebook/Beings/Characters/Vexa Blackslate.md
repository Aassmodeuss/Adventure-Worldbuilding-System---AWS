#### [Character]
**Name:** Vexa Blackslate  

**Aliases:** Chieftain, Chief 
**Summary:** Baned leader of Ash Hollow who sets water tariffs and enforces ration law to keep the spring alive.  
**Full Description:** Vexa bears clear Baned scarring along her neck and forearms, held in check by talismans and bitter salves. She speaks in the old Varden dialect and issues slate-etched decrees that post rates, quotas, and penalties. She shuts taps when reserves fall, exiles siphoners, and trades water access for tools, filters, and armed escorts. Caravans respect her ledgers because she balances survival over profit and backs words with ready guards.  
**Tone Keywords:** austere, pragmatic, scarred, authority, ration-law
