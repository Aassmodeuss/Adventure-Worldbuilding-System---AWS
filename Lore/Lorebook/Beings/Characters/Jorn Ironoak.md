#### [Character]
**Name:** Jorn  Ironoak
**Aliases:** None recorded  
**Summary:** Kitsune sentinel of Westgrove who runs the outer watch and keeps order at the canopy gates.  
**Full Description:** Tall for a Kitsune, Jorn manages entry logs and questions visitors at Westgrove's gates. He reads scent and intentions easily, de-escalates first, and strikes clean when needed. Off duty he observes in silence, writes brief notes, and trains new lookouts. He is lives within Westgrove and reports to  Lethra Vinecaller, of the Westwood Scouts.  
**Tone Keywords:** vigilant, pragmatic, disciplined, perceptive, quiet  
**Known Associates:**  Westwood Druid Circle  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Assist Jorn in screening a suspicious party, choose deny entry or escorted passage.  
- Run a night watch with Jorn, map blind angles at the gate spans.  
- Deliver a sealed note to the Circle on his behalf, accept no questions asked.
