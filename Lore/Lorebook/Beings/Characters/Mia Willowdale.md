#### [Character]
**Name:** Mia Willowdale  
**Aliases:** None recorded  
**Summary:** A 22-year-old Kitsune from Westgrove, Mia grew beside Kieara as sister, then cousin. She mediates family fractures with patience and sharp memory.  
**Full Description:** Daughter of Lyra and Zarun, Mia was born months before Kieara and became her closest companion. She favors practical clothes, quick climbs, and old trails through the canopy. When the truth of Kieara's birth emerged, Mia absorbed the shock and kept both bonds, still referring to her as sister. She prefers to de-escalate fights, remembers details others miss, and manages delicate errands for the Grove.  
**Tone Keywords:** steady, loyal, observant, conciliatory, deft  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Ask Mia to broker a meeting with Lyra or Kieara under safe terms.  
- Follow Mia's notes to navigate a canopy shortcut around Shadowlands patrols.  
- Trade a favor for her quiet help retrieving a keepsake tied to Zarun.
