#### [Character]
**Name:** Lyra Oakenheart  
**Aliases:** None recorded  
**Summary:** Former Foxfire operative who left the fight after Zarun's death to raise her daughter, Mia, within the safety of Westgrove, and adopt her niece, Kieara. Lyra keeps the past buried to protect the present.  
**Full Description:** A Kitsune with steady hands and sharper instincts, Lyra served as scout and courier during Malakai's rise. After Elowyn's death she took in infant Kieara, passing her off as a second daughter to shield her from blame and grief. She delayed the truth until Kieara turned thirteen, then told it all in one night that ended with broken trust. Lyra remains wary of new causes, guarding family above any banner.  
**Tone Keywords:** protective, secretive, pragmatic, maternal, wary  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Reconcile Lyra and Kieara by presenting a balanced record of Elowyn's actions.  
- Courier a hidden Foxfire ledger Lyra still keeps locked away.  
- Ask Lyra to broker contact with Westgrove under strict conditions.
