#### [Character]
**Name:** Elerin Oak  
**Aliases:** Elder Oak, First Councillor  
**Summary:** Millennia-old druid and chief councillor of the Westwood Circle. Elerin holds the Circle to long horizons, choosing slow strength over brittle victories.  
**Full Description:** Elerin reads forest health like a net of pulse and breath, tracing consequences through seasons. He chairs council with calm authority, curbing rash plans while sanctioning necessary risks. His magic anchors groves, firming roots and guiding growth after fire or rot. He keeps the Circle focused on survival rather than pride.  
**Tone Keywords:** patient, strategic, anchoring, measured, elder  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Negotiate resource allocations with Elerin for a multi-season restoration plan.  
- Learn a root-anchoring rite that stabilizes structures grown from living wood.  
- Present evidence to shift the council toward or away from a high-risk purge.
