#### [Character]
**Name:** Diana Stormforge  
**Aliases:** None recorded  
**Summary:** A young Stormpeak dwarf apprenticed to Master Artificer Dura Ironsights, Diana excels at precision craft and bolter maintenance. Quiet in crowds, relentless at a bench.  
**Full Description:** Eighteen years old, she wears her blonde hair in two work-braids and keeps a heavy dagger at her belt. Thin by clan standards, she favors fitted leathers stained with oil and flux. Diana studies metallurgy, alchemy, and gear-work under Dura's strict schedule, logging tolerances others ignore. Her mother died after a long illness; her father vanished soon after, placing her into formal apprenticeship at thirteen like other orphans of the Hold. She shoots the Dwarven bolter with great skill.  
**Tone Keywords:** meticulous, reserved, steadfast, apprentice, loss  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**  
- Assist with a rush repair on a seized gear train in a sealed level, earn her trust.  
- Source a rare alloy from river traders to complete a prototype firing spring.  
- Train with Diana on the range to unlock bolter maintenance perks.
