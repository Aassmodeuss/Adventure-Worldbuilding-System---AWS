# Template - Role / Class

Purpose
- Use for roles, callings, professions, orders, and playable classes.
- Define capabilities, training, costs/limits, social stance, and typical affiliations.

Frontmatter
```yaml
---
id: class:purger
type: class
name: Purger
see_also: []
---
```

Card template
```markdown
[ENTRY: {Name}]

#### [Class]
**Name:**  
**Aliases:**  
**Summary:** 1-2 sentences defining the role/class and its primary purpose. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Full Description.  
**Full Description:** 4-10 sentences on origin/training, hallmark abilities, resource demands, risks/costs, social standing, typical operations, and limits. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Summary.  
**Tone Keywords:** 3-5 anchors.  
**Role Domain:** martial, arcane, divine, craft, civic, etc.  
**Typical Training:** how candidates are selected and trained.  
**Core Abilities:** concise list of defining techniques or powers.  
**Costs and Limits:** metabolic, material, legal, and moral constraints.  
**Common Equipment:** tools, weapons, armor, foci, kits.  
**Aligned Factions or Orders:** comma-separated faction/order names.  
**Notable Members:** comma-separated character card names.  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:** 1-3 concise bullets with concrete stakes tied to this role.  



[END: {Name}]
```

Pre-save checklist
- Role Domain, Typical Training, Core Abilities, and Costs/Limits populated.  
- Aligned Factions/Orders and Notable Members linked when applicable.  
- Wrappers and Name field match exactly.
