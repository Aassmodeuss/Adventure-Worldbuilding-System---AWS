# Template - Object: Story Object

Purpose
- Use for unique or setting-significant artifacts, installations, relics, or devices with narrative importance.
- Capture origin, purpose, operation, stewardship, history, current status, and risks.

Frontmatter
```yaml
---
id: object:the-shroud-tower
type: object
name: The Shroud Tower
see_also: []
---
```

Card template
```markdown
[ENTRY: {Name}]

#### [Object - Story]
**Name:**  
**Aliases:**  
**Summary:** 1-2 sentences stating what the object is, why it exists, and why it matters now. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Full Description.  
**Full Description:** 5-12 sentences on origin and construction, intended function, operating principles, control systems/stewardship, notable historical phases, current status, and known risks/limits. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Summary.  
**Tone Keywords:** 3-5 anchors.  
**Object Class:** artifact, installation, relic, prototype, megastructure, etc.  
**Origin and Makers:** who designed/built it and when.  
**Operating Principles:** concise mechanism/process overview.  
**Control and Access:** who governs/maintains/uses it.  
**Status and Condition:** operational state, degradation, modifications.  
**Known Risks and Limits:** failure modes, hazards, legal constraints.  
**Related Characters:** comma-separated character card names.  
**Related Factions:** comma-separated faction card names.  
**Related Places:** comma-separated place card names.  
**Related History:** comma-separated history card names.  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:** 1-3 concise bullets tied to activation, sabotage, repair, or contested control.  


[END: {Name}]
```


Pre-save checklist
- Object Class, Origin and Makers, and Operating Principles populated.  
- Control/Access, Status/Condition, and Known Risks/Limits stated concretely.  
- Related Characters/Factions/Places/History linked where applicable.  
- Wrappers and Name field match exactly.
