# Template - Object: Weapon

Purpose
- Use for weapons and heavy weapons: melee, ranged, thrown, arcane implements, and heavy platforms.
- Describe construction, mechanism, ammo/power, operation, effects, limits, hazards, and upkeep.

Frontmatter
```yaml
---
id: object:m920-cain
type: object
name: M-920 Cain
see_also: []
---
```

Card template
```markdown
[ENTRY: {Name}]

#### [Object - Weapon]
**Name:**  
**Aliases:**  
**Summary:** 1-2 sentences stating what the weapon is and its primary effect or role. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Full Description.  
**Full Description:** 4-10 sentences on construction, mechanism of action, ammunition/power, operation/charge, effects on impact, maintenance, and limits/risks. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Summary.  
**Tone Keywords:** 3-5 anchors.  
**Weapon Class:** melee, sidearm, rifle, launcher, heavy weapon, focus, etc.  
**Ammunition/Power Source:** fuels, cells, charges, reagents.  
**Mechanism of Action:** how it produces damage or effects.  
**Rate of Fire/Charge:** sustained fire, burst, single-shot, charge time.  
**Effective Range:** typical engagement distances or constraints.  
**Handling/Ergonomics:** weight, recoil, stabilization, user training.  
**Maintenance/Storage:** cleaning, refabrication, charging, shelf life.  
**Safety/Regulation:** hazards, restrictions, export controls.  
**Aligned Factions or Makers:** comma-separated names (if associated).  
**Known Users:** comma-separated character card names (if notable).  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:** 1-3 concise bullets tied to acquisition, ammunition scarcity, calibration, or interdiction.  


[END: {Name}]
```

Pre-save checklist
- Weapon Class, Ammunition/Power Source, and Mechanism of Action populated.  
- Rate of Fire/Charge and Effective Range stated when applicable.  
- Maintenance/Storage and Safety/Regulation list concrete costs/limits.  
- Related makers/users linked when applicable.  
- Wrappers and Name field match exactly.
