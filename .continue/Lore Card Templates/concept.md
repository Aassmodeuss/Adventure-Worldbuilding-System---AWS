# Template - Concept

Purpose
- Use for systemic ideas, processes, phenomena, doctrines, or technologies that recur across the setting.
- Define what it is, how it works, how to recognize it, and how it changes decisions in play.

Frontmatter
```yaml
---
id: concept:indoctrination
type: concept
name: Indoctrination
see_also: []
---
```

Card template
```markdown
[ENTRY: {Name}]

#### [Concept]
**Name:**  
**Aliases:**  
**Summary:** 1-2 sentences that define the concept and why it matters now. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Full Description.  
**Full Description:** 4-10 sentences covering definition, mechanism/process, tell-tale signs, spread/vectors, impacts, and known limits. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Summary.  
**Tone Keywords:** 3-5 anchors.  
**Domains/Scope:** list affected areas (e.g., Magic, Corruption, Society, Warfare).  
**Tell-tale Signs:** brief cues that reveal presence or escalation.  
**Impacts:** 2-4 concise lines describing practical effects on factions, travel, combat, or governance.  
**Countermeasures and Limits:** specific mitigations, costs, and failure points.  
**Related Characters:** comma-separated character card names (if central to this concept).  
**Related Factions:** comma-separated faction card names.  
**Related Races:** comma-separated race card names.  
**Related Places:** comma-separated place card names.  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:** 1-3 concise bullets tying the concept to immediate stakes.  

 

[END: {Name}]
```


Pre-save checklist
- Summary defines the concept and its present impact.  
- Full Description covers mechanism, signs, impacts, and limits.  
- Related links populated where applicable.  
- Wrappers and Name field match exactly.
