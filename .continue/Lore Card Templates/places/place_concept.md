# Template - Place Concept

Purpose
- Use for geographic umbrella ideas like Forsaken Lands, High Road, Shardfall Sites.  
- Exists outside the strict spatial tree; used for parallel linking.

Frontmatter
```yaml
---
id: concept:forsaken-lands
type: place-concept
name: Forsaken Lands
---
```

Card template
```markdown
[ENTRY: {Name}]

#### [Concept]
**Name:**  
**Summary:** Definition and scope of the concept. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Full Description.  
**Full Description:** 3-5 sentences on how it is used in practice, typical impacts, and references to forms. Write this as a standalone section; do not rely on Summary.  
**Tone Keywords:** 3-5 anchors.  
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:** 1-3 concise bullets, concept-driven tasks.  



[END: {Name}]
```

Pre-save checklist
- No parent.  
- Keep wording neutral and widely applicable.  
- Ensure Links point to representative pages.
