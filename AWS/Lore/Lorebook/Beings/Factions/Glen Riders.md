#### [Faction]
**Name:** Glen Riders
**Aliases:** Rustwalkers, Oilbound
**Summary:** The Glen Riders are a faction of cyborg outcasts, marked by rusted limbs and leaking oil. They roam the lowlands, scavenging for lost technology.
**Full Description:** Glen Riders are a faction known for their broken cybernetic enhancements and patchwork bodies. Many Glen Riders bear visible signs of decay, with rusted plating and exposed wiring. Their origins trace to failed augmentation experiments in the industrial valleys. The Glen Riders survive by salvaging machinery and trading in rare mechanical parts. Oil stains and mechanical whirring often mark their passage through the glens. Despite their condition, Glen Riders maintain a strict code of loyalty to their own. The faction is often encountered near abandoned factories and scrapyards.
**Tone Keywords:** cyborg, broken, rust, oil
**Aligned Characters:** Mira the Welded, Scraphand Jorik
**Known Locations:** Iron Glen, The Sump Fields
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**
- A Glen Riders convoy breaks down, blocking a vital trade route
- Rival factions seek to exploit Glen Riders' mechanical expertise
- A surge in oil thefts traced to Glen Riders territory
