#### [Faction]
**Name:** Wolf Pack
**Aliases:** The Howling Tribe, Flesh-Eaters
**Summary:** Wolf Pack is a tribal faction known for its beastmaster traditions and cannibalistic rituals. The group is feared for its ferocity and insular culture.
**Full Description:** Wolf Pack operates as a fiercely independent tribal faction, maintaining strict traditions centered around beast mastery and survival. Members of Wolf Pack are recognized for their ability to control wild animals and for practicing ritual cannibalism as part of their rites. The faction is organized into tightly knit hunting bands that rarely interact with outsiders. Wolf Pack's leadership is determined by displays of strength and cunning, often through brutal contests. Their territory is marked by animal totems and warning signs to deter intruders. Wolf Pack's presence is a constant threat to neighboring settlements due to their unpredictable raids and resource competition.
**Tone Keywords:** beastmaster, cannibal, tribal
**Aligned Characters:** Redmaw, Shira the Tamer
**Known Locations:** Bloodpine Forest, The Maw Caves
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**
- Territorial disputes escalate between Wolf Pack and nearby villages
- A rare beast controlled by Wolf Pack threatens trade routes
- Internal leadership struggle destabilizes Wolf Pack's hunting bands
