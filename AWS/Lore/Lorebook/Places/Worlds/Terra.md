#### [World]
**Name:** Terra
**Summary:** In the far future, mankind has turned Terra, their birthplace, into a desolate wasteland. Few remnants of civilization remain amid the devastation.
**Full Description:** Terra is a world marked by the scars of human ambition and neglect. Once vibrant, Terra now endures as a barren wasteland shaped by centuries of conflict and environmental collapse. The last inhabitants of Terra, remnants of mankind, struggle to survive among ruins and toxic landscapes. Ancient cities like the Ashen Spires and the Vault of Echoes stand as silent witnesses to lost glory. The atmosphere is harsh and resources are scarce, forcing adaptation and resilience. Terra's surface is dotted with hazardous zones such as the Glassed Barrens and the Ironbone Expanse. The world is haunted by memories of its past and the consequences of irreversible change. Terra remains a stark reminder of the cost of unchecked progress.
**Local Inhabitants:** Mankind
**Tone Keywords:** far future, loss, wasteland, apocalypse
**Locations of Note:** Ashen Spires, Vault of Echoes, Glassed Barrens, Ironbone Expanse
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**
- Toxic storms threaten the last habitable enclaves
- Ancient machines awaken beneath the Ashen Spires
- Resource scarcity drives conflict among isolated survivors
