#### [Region]
**Name:** The Sea of Glass
**Summary:** In the jagged wasteland of The Sea of Glass, the Glen Riders and the Wolf Pack Gang vie for control over Ash Haven.
**Full Description:** The Sea of Glass is a parched and shattered region marked by endless fields of scorched, fused earth. Jagged shards of glass rise from the ground, making travel perilous and slow. The Glen Riders and the Wolf Pack Gang are the primary inhabitants, locked in a bitter struggle for dominance. Ash Haven stands as the only significant settlement, offering a rare refuge amid the emptiness. The environment is harsh, with relentless heat and scarce water shaping daily survival. The Sea of Glass remains a place of constant tension, where alliances shift and violence is common. Few outsiders dare to cross its boundaries, fearing both the land and its inhabitants.
**Local Inhabitants:** Glen Riders, Wolf Pack Gang
**Tone Keywords:** shattered, empty, scorching, parched, jagged
**Locations of Note:** Ash Haven
**Potential Gameplay Hooks:**
- A sudden drought intensifies conflict over Ash Haven's dwindling water supply
- A glass storm isolates Ash Haven, forcing uneasy truces among rivals
- A hidden cache beneath the glass draws scavengers and sparks violence
